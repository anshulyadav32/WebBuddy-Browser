# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 18, 2026

WebBuddy Browser ("WebBuddy", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process, how it is used, and your choices.

## 1. What WebBuddy Stores

WebBuddy is designed to keep browser data on your device.

Depending on features you use, WebBuddy may store:
- Browsing history
- Bookmarks
- Open tabs and session state
- Download metadata
- Settings (theme, search engine, homepage, privacy preferences)
- Cached website content and cookies (via WebView)
- Offline pages you explicitly save
- Shield/privacy counters and site preferences

This data is stored locally on your device storage.

## 2. Data We Do Not Sell

We do not sell your personal data.

## 3. Network Requests and Third Parties

When you browse, websites and services you visit can receive your IP address and standard browser/network metadata.

WebBuddy may interact with third-party services based on your usage, such as:
- Search providers you select
- Websites you visit
- Android/iOS/Windows WebView components

Their own privacy policies apply to that processing.

## 4. Permissions

WebBuddy may request permissions only when needed for features (for example, downloads or file access). You can deny permissions, but related features may not work.

## 5. Cookies, Storage, and Tracking

Websites loaded in WebView may use cookies and local storage.

WebBuddy provides controls to:
- Clear browsing data
- Configure cookie behavior
- Manage site settings and privacy options

## 6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards for local data handling. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

WebBuddy is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## 8. Your Choices

You can:
- Clear local browsing data in Settings
- Delete bookmarks/history/download records
- Change privacy settings at any time
- Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data (subject to platform behavior)

## 9. International Users

If you use WebBuddy in different regions, local laws may provide additional rights.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. Updates will be reflected by a new effective date.
